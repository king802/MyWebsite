Who Am I?

Hello, my name is Max King! I was introduced to programming during the last few years of completing my undergraduate degree in mechanical engineering. I immediately fell in love and decided upon the completion of my degree to go back to school to get my master's in Computer Science. In the time since I was introduced to the world of computer science till now, I have worked on all sorts of different projects and I decided to put this website together to try and capture that work and share it!

I have a wide range of interest and I hope that this website is able to organize them and present them in a meaningful way. I have added my work that I completed while in undergrad and graduate school as well as some of my own personal projects.
